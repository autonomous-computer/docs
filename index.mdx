# OMNI Datastream Docs

## Three ways in

- Datastream Docs: use-case guides, workflow recipes, and copyable prompts for coding agents
- API Reference: exact endpoint contracts, headers, examples, and failure posture
- Libraries & SDKs: install, auth, retries, MCP, CLI, and language-specific usage

## What OMNI Datastream is

OMNI Datastream is the primary-source public market intelligence layer for OMNI. It is built for investor agents, internal tools, and product teams that need grounded SEC and market workflows without hauling around raw filing sprawl.

## Core OMNI Datastream 3.0 capabilities

- canonical entity resolution for issuers and filers
- historical filing search with accession reads
- filing section and item extraction
- statements, facts, and all-statements bundles
- offerings, ownership, insider, and beneficial ownership workflows
- market calendar, rights-safe index constituents, and OMNI Volatility Score
- provenance, freshness, and materialization metadata on public contracts
