# Plans and Pricing

## Default motion

- every new organization starts with `250` included calls
- Pay As You Go is the default paid path
- Personal and Team are self-serve commit plans
- Commercial is the first plan with redistribution and external commercial rights

## Launch plans

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pay As You Go" icon="gauge">

  <Card title="Personal" icon="user">

## Meter-family launch pricing

- `light_reads`: `$0.01` ‚Äî entity resolution and lightweight metadata
- `standard_reads`: `$0.02` ‚Äî filings, facts, statements, ownership, compensation
- `heavy_extracts`: `$0.05` ‚Äî section extraction and comparison workflows
- `artifact_jobs`: `$0.10` ‚Äî artifact generation and export jobs
- `delivery_events`: `$0.001` ‚Äî webhook and stream delivery
- `intelligence_queries`: `$0.08` ‚Äî semantic intelligence, country reports, portfolio analysis, strategy queries
- `market_data_reads`: `$0.03` ‚Äî factor returns, macro indicators, market snapshots

## Monthly AI-query allowance

The `ai_queries` quota covers semantic intelligence + AI-graded routes (`/v1/intelligence/*`, `GET /v1/signals/dilution/enhanced`, plus future chat surfaces). Resets on the first day of each UTC calendar month.

| Plan | Monthly AI queries |
| --- | --- |
| Sandbox grant | 10 (trial) |
| Pay As You Go | 0 (charges per call) |
| Personal | **600** |
| Team | **3,000** |
| Commercial | **10,000** (per-org override available) |

Every quota-counted response carries `Omni-AI-Quota-Limit`, `Omni-AI-Quota-Used`, `Omni-AI-Quota-Remaining`, and `Omni-AI-Quota-Reset` headers. `GET /v1/billing` returns the same numbers under `monthlyQuotas.ai_queries`.

## Rights boundary

- internal evaluation: starter grant
- internal use: PAYG, Personal, Team
- redistribution, embedding, white-label, resale: Commercial
