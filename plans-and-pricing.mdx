# Plans and Pricing

## Default motion

- every new organization starts with `100` included calls
- Pay As You Go is the default paid path
- Personal and Team are self-serve commit plans
- Commercial is the first plan with redistribution and external commercial rights

## Launch plans

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pay As You Go" icon="gauge">
    No fixed monthly fee. Highest per-call pricing. Best for low-friction onboarding and real-world evaluation after the starter grant.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personal" icon="user">
    `$99/mo` or `$1,068/yr`. Lower unit pricing for a single analyst or developer who has repeated usage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team" icon="users">
    `$399/mo` or `$4,068/yr`. Shared-org plan for up to five seats with higher throughput and more keys.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Commercial" icon="building-2">
    From `$18,000/yr`. Redistribution rights, contract handling, invoicing, and deeper onboarding.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Meter-family launch pricing

- `light_reads`: `$0.01`
- `standard_reads`: `$0.02`
- `heavy_extracts`: `$0.05`
- `artifact_jobs`: `$0.10`
- `delivery_events`: `$0.001`

## Rights boundary

- internal evaluation: starter grant
- internal use: PAYG, Personal, Team
- redistribution, embedding, white-label, resale: Commercial
