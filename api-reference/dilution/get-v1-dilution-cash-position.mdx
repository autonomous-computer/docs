## Evidence and limits

Cash, burn, and runway fields are based on reported data and stated assumptions in covered filings. They are not a live treasury balance, a forecast, or a financing recommendation. Preserve the reporting period, source, and verification, then compare like-for-like periods before calculating runway. See [filing and source conventions](/api-conventions).
