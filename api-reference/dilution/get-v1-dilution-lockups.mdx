Find disclosed lockup restrictions, dates, parties, and release conditions

<Info>
Audience: investors and developers tracking disclosed resale restrictions.
</Info>

## Canonical metadata

- `data`
- `hasMore`
- `nextCursor`
- `per-row: startDate, endDate, sharesAmount, conditions, verification.confidence`
- `requestId`
- `traceparent (standard response/header)`

## Example request

<RequestExample>
```bash
curl -X GET -H "x-api-key: $SECAPI_API_KEY" -H "secapi-version: 2026-03-19" "https://api.secapi.ai/v1/dilution/lockups?ticker=EXAMPLE&limit=10"
```
</RequestExample>

## Evidence and limits

Lockup dates, parties, and conditions are disclosure-derived. They can be amended, waived, or subject to exceptions, so a listed end date is not proof that shares became saleable or were sold. Carry the cited filing and verification fields into any calendar or alert. See [coverage and depth](/coverage-and-depth).

## Failure posture

- retain parties, conditions, source accession, and verification
- treat lockup expiry as a research cue, not evidence of a sale
- verify amendments and exceptions in the underlying filing
