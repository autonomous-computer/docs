## Evidence and limits

These rows surface provisions disclosed in covered underwriter or financing materials. A right of first refusal or tail provision is a contractual term, not evidence that a follow-on financing occurred or will occur. Review the cited agreement and any amendment for scope and expiry. See [filing and source conventions](/api-conventions).
