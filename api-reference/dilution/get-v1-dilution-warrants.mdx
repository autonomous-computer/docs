## Evidence and limits

Warrant terms are extracted from filed materials and may omit later exercises, cashless-election outcomes, amendments, or instruments outside the current coverage. Preserve exercise price, underlying shares, anti-dilution terms, accession, and verification with any analysis. Potential shares are not issued shares. See [coverage and depth](/coverage-and-depth) before treating a result set as complete.

## Failure posture

- keep source accession and verification with each warrant
- distinguish potential underlying shares from exercised or outstanding common shares
- check later filings before relying on a term as current
