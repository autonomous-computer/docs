## Evidence and limits

Reverse-split rows record disclosed ratios and execution dates. They help normalize historical share counts, but do not independently explain price performance, listing status, or later issuance. Use the cited filing and [share-float history](/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-share-float-history) when reconciling a time series.
