# Dilution

Reference pages for Dilution endpoints.

<Note>
The sidebar labels inside this family are the literal calls you make over HTTP. Descriptions stay in the body so the navigation stays sharp and scannable.
</Note>

## Operations in this family

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/cash-position" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-cash-position">Cash runway, burn, and management commentary computed from quarterly filings</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/convertibles" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-convertibles">List convertible debt schedules with conversion price, maturity, and ratchet metadata</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/corporate-actions" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-corporate-actions">List dilution-relevant corporate actions: ticker changes, exchange moves, de-SPAC closings, splits</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/coverage" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-coverage">Dilution-domain coverage and freshness summary across forms, tickers, and verification status</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/events" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-events">List dilution events (S-1, 424B*, FWP, S-3 offerings) with filters by ticker, form, date, and is_atm</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/events/{event_id}" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-events-event-id">Retrieve a single dilution event with verification block, warrants, and convertibles linkage</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/lockups" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-lockups">List lockup schedules with start/end dates, parties, and conditions</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/nasdaq-compliance" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-nasdaq-compliance">List Nasdaq deficiency notices with status and remediation tracking</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/ratings" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-ratings">Composite Dilution Score with sub-factors (offering ability, historical, cash need, warrant exercise risk)</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/reverse-splits" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-reverse-splits">List reverse-stock-split actions with execution date and ratio</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/rofr" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-rofr">List right-of-first-refusal and tail-financing clauses parsed from underwriter agreements</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/score" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-score">Per-issuer Dilution Score with explanation and source events (alias of ratings, scoring-page primary entrypoint)</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/share-float-history" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-share-float-history">Historical shares outstanding and public float series materialized from filings</Card>
<Card title="GET /v1/dilution/warrants" href="/api-reference/dilution/get-v1-dilution-warrants">List warrant schedules parsed from offering exhibits with price-protection and ratchet clauses</Card>
</CardGroup>
