Use this registry when a response is more than a source document. It distinguishes what a derived result describes from what it cannot establish. The route reference defines fields and parameters; this page is an interpretation guide, not a replacement contract.

```bash
curl -sS -H "x-api-key: $SECAPI_API_KEY" \
  "https://api.secapi.ai/v1/market/calendar?market=XNYS"
```

For consequential output, retain the route, parameters, period, source fields, methodology version when supplied, and every returned capability, warning, degradation, or `statusNote` field.

## Shared terms

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Source-backed | Tied to a disclosed filing, an official public source, or both. |
| Derived | Normalized, classified, compared, or calculated from source material. |
| `capability` | A route-specific availability state, not a platform-wide quality grade. |
| `statusNote` | A nullable explanation for a limited, partial, or unavailable result. |

## Market schedules and index data

For `GET /v1/market/calendar`, read `coverage`, `confidence`, `statusNote`, and `sessionStatus` together. `configured_holidays` means the requested market has configured holiday coverage. `weekend_only` does not establish local-holiday coverage. `tentative` means the route cannot confirm the day from configured coverage. `XNYS` and `XNAS` have configured holiday coverage.

Index inventory and constituent routes are subject to their returned rights posture. `inventory_only` does not make a constituent roster public. A `null` weight or `unpublished` weight method is not a portfolio weight, and `positionRank` is ordering rather than a weight.

## Filing-backed and derived results

| Surface | Treat it as | Do not treat it as |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Facts and statements | normalized issuer disclosures with period and unit context | a universal cross-issuer taxonomy |
| M&A events | filing-derived event records | a complete deal database or live closing feed |
| Risk categories | deterministic coverage for the documented section | a complete issuer risk assessment |
| Volatility score | a disclosure-derived signal with methodology and limitations | a forecast or investment recommendation |
| Enforcement actions | official SEC release records | a replacement for the linked source in a legal conclusion |

Anchor filing-backed results to an accession number and filing URL. A page-level trace is page-level evidence; it does not prove an exact resolved source span. Segment labels and hierarchies retain the issuer's disclosure context.

Show unknown, partial, degraded, and unavailable states in your application. Pair this page with [Freshness and source evidence](/freshness-and-trust) and [Coverage and historical depth](/coverage-and-depth) before treating a calculation as decision-ready.
