title: "Filing content search"
description: "Search filing text and sections, then verify each result against the cited filing source"
---

Search filing content when the question is about language inside a filing. It narrows a corpus to candidate passages; the returned accession number and section are an evidence handoff, not a substitute for reading the source.

## Search semantically, then fetch the section

```bash
curl -sS \
  -H "x-api-key: $SECAPI_API_KEY" \
  "https://api.secapi.ai/v1/search/semantic?q=supply%20chain%20risk&ticker=AAPL&form=10-K&mode=hybrid&limit=10"
```

Semantic results can include `accession`, `section_key`, `source_url`, `char_start`, `char_end`, and `highlighted_snippet`. Record those fields with `requestId`, then fetch the selected section before quoting or summarizing its language.

## Retrieval routes

| Method | Path | Useful inputs | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `GET` | [`/v1/search/fulltext`](/api-reference/search/get-v1-search-fulltext) | Required `q`; issuer, form, accession, date, and fiscal filters. | Filing and section matches for a text query. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/search/semantic`](/api-reference/search/get-v1-search-semantic) | Required `q`; `mode=keyword`, `semantic`, or `hybrid`; issuer and filing filters. | Section matches with citation fields. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/sections/search`](/api-reference/filings) | Filing-scoped filters and query inputs. | Section and snippet matches. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/filings/{accession_number}/sections/{section_key}`](/api-reference/filings) | Selected accession and section key. | The source section to inspect or cite. |

`hybrid` is the default semantic mode. Scope a query with ticker, CIK, form, accession number, filing date, fiscal year, or filing year when the research needs a bounded corpus.

## Citation quality and availability

Citation spans and highlighted snippets can be unavailable. When `_citation_degraded` is present, keep the accession and section identity and retrieve the source rather than relying on the excerpt. Full-text search requires its configured search capability; semantic search also depends on vector search. An unavailable dependency can return `503`, not an invented match.

Ranking helps retrieval. It does not prove a result is complete, material, legally dispositive, or the best interpretation of a disclosure.

## Next action

Retrieve the selected section, retain its filing URL and period, and compare the original wording before presenting it as evidence.
