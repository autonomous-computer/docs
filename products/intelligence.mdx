---
title: "Intelligence workflows"
description: "Run defined company, security, portfolio, macro, and footnote research jobs with source-aware outputs"
---

Intelligence routes assemble a defined research job into one response or a queued result. Use them for a target or structured request that needs a company brief, security bundle, portfolio analysis, macro exposure, country report, or filing-footnote investigation with its source context intact.

## Start with an issuer bundle

```bash
curl -sS \
  -H "x-api-key: $SECAPI_API_KEY" \
  "https://api.secapi.ai/v1/intelligence/company?ticker=AAPL"
```

Company and security requests require a ticker or CIK. Record returned traces, source references, freshness metadata, warnings, and `requestId`; an omitted field can reflect available supporting data, not a negative conclusion.

## Routes by research job

| Method | Path | Job | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `GET` | [`/v1/intelligence/company`](/api-reference/intelligence) | Company context for a ticker or CIK. | A composed company response. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/intelligence/security`](/api-reference/intelligence) | Security context and catalysts. | A security bundle. |
| `POST` | [`/v1/intelligence/query`](/api-reference/intelligence) | Submit a documented intelligence request. | A completed result or queued job. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/intelligence/query/{jobId}`](/api-reference/intelligence) | Poll a queued request. | Current job state or completed result. |
| `POST` | [`/v1/intelligence/portfolio`](/api-reference/intelligence) | Analyze a documented portfolio body. | Portfolio intelligence output. |
| `POST` | [`/v1/intelligence/watchlist`](/api-reference/intelligence) | Brief a documented watchlist body. | Watchlist output. |
| `POST` | [`/v1/intelligence/country-report`](/api-reference/intelligence/post-v1-intelligence-country-report) | Build a country research bundle. | Country report output. |
| `POST` | [`/v1/intelligence/macro-exposure`](/api-reference/intelligence/post-v1-intelligence-macro-exposure) | Calculate portfolio macro sensitivities. | Macro-exposure output. |
| `POST` | [`/v1/intelligence/footnotes/query`](/api-reference/intelligence) | Investigate filing footnotes. | A result or collection. |

For POST routes, use the endpoint reference for the exact JSON body. `POST /v1/intelligence/query` returns `200` for a completed result and `202` for a queued job. Poll the returned job ID to a final state, and handle `202`, `404`, and `502` as distinct outcomes.

## What the response can and cannot prove

An intelligence bundle is a composed research output, not an independent source of record. Coverage and freshness depend on the supporting data planes and supplied body. Follow source references and filing identities for a decision-critical fact, and do not infer an unavailable signal from an omitted field or unfinished job.

## Next action

For a completed result, attach its trace and cited sources to the research record. For a queued result, poll the job endpoint and retain its final response rather than treating the acceptance response as analysis.
