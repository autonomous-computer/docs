---
title: "Filing intelligence"
description: "Use filing events, diffs, transcripts, and guidance as source-linked signals for an SEC filing review workflow."
---

Filing intelligence provides derived views over SEC filing material to help developers find and prioritize the source filing that deserves review.

## What you can do

- Compare two filings of the same form, with automatic latest-versus-prior pairing when no selectors are supplied.
- Query persisted filing events, earnings transcripts, and structured guidance rows by ticker, CIK, date, and documented filters.
- Obtain a single-issuer coverage report across the persisted filing-intelligence stores when that plane is enabled.
- Carry each row's filing identity and source URL into a source-document retrieval step.

## Endpoints

| Method | Path | What it returns |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `GET` | [`/v1/filings/diff`](/api-reference/filings/get-v1-filings-diff) | A section-level comparison for one issuer and form. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/filings/events`](/api-reference/filings) | Persisted filing-event rows. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/filings/transcripts`](/api-reference/filings) | Transcript rows derived from qualifying earnings materials. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/filings/guidance`](/api-reference/filings) | Structured guidance rows derived from earnings materials and transcripts. |
| `GET` | [`/v1/intelligence/coverage`](/api-reference/intelligence) | An issuer coverage rollup. |

## Example

```bash
curl -sS \
  -H "x-api-key: $SECAPI_API_KEY" \
  "https://api.secapi.ai/v1/filings/diff?ticker=AAPL&form=10-K&sections=item_1a_risk_factors,item_7_mdna"
```

`ticker` and `form` are required for a diff. Without `from` and `to`, the API pairs the latest filing with the prior filing of that form. Supply accession numbers or ISO dates when the comparison pair must be explicit.

## Boundaries to keep in view

- Events, transcript rows, guidance rows, and diffs are derived output. They direct attention; they do not establish legal, accounting, or investment conclusions.
- Transcript coverage is limited to qualifying earnings materials. No transcript or guidance row is not evidence that no call or guidance existed.
- A diff describes only its selected pair, not every disclosure change by an issuer.
- These routes can return `404` when the filing-intelligence plane is not enabled. Treat that as availability, not an empty result.
