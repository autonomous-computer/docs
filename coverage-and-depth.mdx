# Coverage and historical depth

<Note>
**Dilution support is live in all 4 SDKs and the CLI (April 2026).** Eleven row types (events, warrants, convertibles, ROFR, lockups, cash position, corporate actions, Nasdaq compliance, ratings, reverse splits, share-float history) plus a coverage rollup and a per-issuer score are exposed through 14 REST endpoints (`/v1/dilution/*`), 14 MCP tools, and first-class methods in the TypeScript, Python, Go, and Rust SDKs. Thirteen of the 14 routes honor `?view=agent`; coverage is already compact. See [Dilution endpoints](/api-reference/dilution).
</Note>

This page summarizes current public coverage without overstating what SEC API has not yet shipped. Current coverage includes:

- broad public workflows built around SEC and adjacent market utilities
- a 9-year searchable history floor for filings and section workflows
- stronger completed-year completeness guarantees for the most recent two full years of filings and section workflows
- a 30-year cold-storage policy target that remains intentionally deferred from the hot path
- explicit partial and unavailable states where support is not fully generalized yet

## XBRL facts coverage

- 95M+ pre-parsed XBRL facts stored in PostgreSQL across 9,600+ SEC-registered companies
- all SEC XBRL taxonomies covered: us-gaap, ifrs-full, dei
- daily automated sync at 07:00 UTC refreshes the full universe
- DB-first query architecture: facts and statement endpoints query our database first, falling back to SEC EDGAR only when data is missing

## Current snapshot

- filing and section history validations currently pass across the 9-year searchable floor
- stronger per-form completeness floors apply to the last two completed years; all 9 searchable years do not carry the same density guarantee
- historical coverage is backed by validation evidence, not a generic “full archive is always hot” claim
- current public workflow coverage includes:
  - entities and canonical resolution
  - filings, latest filing retrieval, and section extraction across 10-K / 10-Q / 8-K / DEF 14A plus offering forms (S-1, S-3, 424B*, FWP, 20-F)
  - normalized statements, facts, segmented revenues (with geographic depth detection and hierarchy filtering), share float, and common equity derivation with preferred stock deduction
  - ownership, insiders, historical institutional extract, and compensation
  - SEC-native M&A events, SEC-furnished earnings materials, enforcement events, and board composition
  - market calendar, rights-scoped indices, and SEC API Volatility Score
  - market data: snapshots, historical bars, corporate actions, reference, estimates, and search
  - factor intelligence: 84-factor catalog across market, style, macro, sector, and industry categories with daily returns, correlations, intraday, dashboard, and regime performance
  - macro intelligence: indicators, releases, calendar, forecasts, high-signal packs (12 countries including 24 daily US FRED series), and regime classification
  - investment intelligence: security, company, earnings preview, portfolio, watchlist, semantic query, and footnote analysis
  - portfolio strategies: factor decomposition, stress testing, optimization, factor rotation, and regime screening

## Partial or intentionally scoped support

- market calendar:
  `XNYS` and `XNAS` are holiday-configured; other covered exchanges remain `weekend_only`
- index constituents:
  `NDX` is public; `SPX`, `DJIA`, and `RUT` remain rights-scoped for public export
- offerings:
  public filing records are live; section-level extraction (Prospectus Summary, Risk Factors, Use of Proceeds, Capitalization, Dilution, Description of Securities, Underwriting, Selling Stockholders, Plan of Distribution, Principal Stockholders, Management, Executive Compensation, Related-Party Transactions, 424B pricing supplements, FWP term sheets, 20-F items) is live for S-1 / S-3 / 424B* / FWP / 20-F with a dedicated catalog and exhibit parsing for EX-4, EX-5, and EX-10 (warrant, legality, and material-contract exhibits). Richer extracted dilution payloads such as warrant terms, lockup schedules, and rights of first refusal are not part of this offering extractor.
- sentiment:
  not public
- traceability:
  filing-derived document-plus-page support is live, HTML-first exact region anchors ship for supported segmented revenue facts and supported section excerpts, and the shared trace API also covers SEC enforcement source records. Broader PDF/cell parity and richer non-filing families are not public yet.
- analytics:
  a scoped query API is live across filings, sections, segmented revenues, SEC enforcement actions, and quarter-specific institutional ownership extracts. Raw SQL and broader warehouse-style coverage are not public.

## Historical depth

- searchable hot coverage today:
  9-year filings and sections floor
- cold archive target:
  30-year archive
- not included today:
  full 30-year hot normalization

Warm and cold tiering remains separate from current production behavior. Treat the 30-year archive as a policy target, not as a hot-path normalization guarantee.
