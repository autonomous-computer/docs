Coverage is not one number. A filing may be searchable while its text, extracted sections, XBRL facts, or a specialized workflow is unavailable or partial. This page states the public boundary so you can design a workflow around what is actually returned.

## Public filing and section history

The checked-in history-quality contract defines:

| Commitment | Scope |
| --- | --- |
| Searchable floor | 9 public years for filing and section workflows |
| Completed-year guarantee | The two most recent completed years have release-blocking completeness checks for the required filing forms |
| Inventory target | 20 years of inventory coverage, which is not the same as a public completeness promise |
| Current year | An availability floor, not a completed-year completeness guarantee |

Filing manifests are distinct from rendered filings, extracted sections, and XBRL facts. Use [Freshness, Coverage, and Source Evidence](/freshness-and-trust) when your workflow needs a current or derived artifact rather than a filing record.

## What the API covers

| Need | Current public surface | Boundary to keep in mind |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Find a filing | Filing search, latest filing, and accession lookup | A manifest does not promise every derived representation is ready. |
| Read a filing or named section | Render and section endpoints for documented forms and section keys | Availability varies by filing format, extraction support, and freshness class. |
| Analyze financial facts | XBRL facts and normalized statements | A database-first path can use an explicit SEC fallback; field availability varies by issuer disclosure. |
| Ownership and insiders | 13F, 13D/13G, and insider workflow families | Verify issuer/date/form coverage for the workflow you are building. |
| Offerings, events, and earnings materials | Documented specialized endpoints | These are scoped derived workflows, not a promise to normalize every exhibit or filing family. |

## Rights and partial surfaces

- `XNYS` and `XNAS` have configured holiday coverage. Other documented markets can be `weekend_only` or `tentative`; read their `coverage`, `confidence`, and status fields.
- Public index-constituent access is rights-scoped. Do not assume an internal mapping audit means every index roster is publishable.
- Some advanced surfaces, including sentiment and selected data rights, are intentionally gated. A route, catalog entry, or internal inventory is not itself a public availability claim.

## What we do not claim

- A universal real-time lag across SEC filing, text, section, statement, and semantic-retrieval classes.
- Equal-density or completeness guarantees for every year outside the documented 9-year searchable floor and two completed-year checks.
- A public normalized parser for every EDGAR exhibit, filing family, or historical document format.
- Current corpus counts without a dated retained production artifact.

## Design a resilient workflow

1. Start with an issuer, form, date, or accession and retrieve the source record.
2. Check the response for the specific derived surface you need rather than assuming it follows the filing manifest.
3. Preserve the source URL, accession, and request ID.
4. Handle documented unavailable or degraded outcomes in code instead of silently substituting an unrelated data source.
